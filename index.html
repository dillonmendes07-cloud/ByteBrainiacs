About Byte Brainiacs

A hackathon born from the belief that the best tech is built by teams who challenge each other to think deeper, ship faster, and dream bigger.

Our Mission

Byte Brainiacs bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering. We create a high-stakes environment where students collaborate and ship something meaningful in 48 hours — whether they arrive as a squad or a stranger.

How It Works

Register
Join as a ready-made team of exactly 3 from the same college, or register solo and enter the waiting pool. Each team receives a unique code.
Team Assignment
Solo participants get grouped into teams of 3 by our admin, prioritising same-college groupings. You'll be notified via email with your team code.
Problem Drop
Receive the problem statement 24 hours before kick-off. Use the warm-up window wisely.
48h Sprint
Build, break, and rebuild your prototype. Mentors are live throughout.
Grand Finale
Top 3 teams present to judges and compete for the championship title and prizes.
